Chicken Chilaquiles Verdes

How to make authentic green chilaquiles!

Just like Mom's best dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of tomatillos
  • 3 to 4 jalapeno pepers
  • 1 garlic clove
  • A pinch of cilantro
  • Fresh onion
  • Tortilla chips
  • Mexican cream
  • 1 cup of mexican crumble cheese
  • Rostisserie style chicken to your liking (shredded)
  • Cooking oil to your liking

Directions:

For the salsa:
  1. Cook in water 1 pound tomatillos with the jalapeno peppers and 1 garlic clove in a covered saucepan. Simmer for about 12 to 15 minutes until soft and tender.
  2. Drain the water, reserving Â¼ of the cooking liquid.
  3. In a blender, puree the peppers, tomatoes, garlic, add the reserved liquid if needed to get a saucier texture. Set aside.
    4. For the chilaquiles
    1. Heat up a pan and add a small ammount of cooking oil
    2. Add the tortilla chips just until they heat up enough
    3. Add the salsa and season with salt
    4. Carefully stir to avoid breaking the tortilla chips
    5. Add the shredded chicken
    6. Serve garnished with crumble cheese, cilantro, onion and topped with the cream.
    7. Avocado is also a good addition to the plate!
